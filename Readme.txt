Notes from the 5 mini-courses offered during the summer school on Gromov-Witten theory at Pingree Park, June-July 2014.

The first week, there are three mini-courses:
Aaron Pixton: The tautological ring and cohomological field theories
Tyler Jarvis: An introduction to FJRW Theory
Ionut Ciocan-Fontanine: Quasimaps and mirror theorems for GIT quotients

The second week, there are two mini-courses:
Jonathan Wise: Deformations, obstructions, and the virtual fundamental class 
Hiroshi Iritani: Toric mirror symmetry

Potentially, these notes could be developed into a proceedings...
